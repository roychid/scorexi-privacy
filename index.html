Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Scorexi ("we", "our", or "us"). Scorexi is a football scores and match tracking application that provides live scores, match timelines, commentary, highlights, and tournament information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Scorexi mobile application. By using Scorexi, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Notification preferences you set within the app
  • Favourite teams and matches you select

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage data (screens viewed, features used)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Firebase analytics data (collected via Google Firebase)

2.3 Information Collected by Third Parties

Scorexi uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID / GAID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device and app identifiers
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google uses this data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Deliver live scores, match updates, and football content
  • Send push notifications for matches and events you follow (only if you have granted permission)
  • Remember your favourite teams and notification preferences
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Send in-app announcements about Scorexi features and content

4. Push Notifications

Scorexi may send push notifications for live match updates, goals, and events for teams you follow. You can manage or disable notifications at any time through:

  • The Scorexi notification settings screen within the app
  • Your device's system notification settings

5. In-App Announcements

Scorexi displays in-app announcements in a banner within the app. These announcements may include:

  • Information about new Scorexi features
  • Links to the Scorexi website for additional content
  • General updates about the app or football coverage

These announcements are controlled by Scorexi and are not third-party advertisements. You can dismiss any announcement by tapping the close button.

6. Advertising

Scorexi displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. These ads help us keep the app free. AdMob may use your advertising ID and other data to show personalised ads based on your interests.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by:

  • Going to your device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • Resetting your Advertising ID in your device settings

7. Data Storage and Firebase

Scorexi uses Google Firebase for the following services:

  • Firebase Authentication — app initialisation
  • Cloud Firestore — storing in-app announcement content
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — delivering push notifications
  • Firebase Analytics — understanding how users interact with the app

Data processed by Firebase is subject to Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Firebase servers may be located outside your country.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Google (Firebase and AdMob) — as described above
  • Our football data API provider — for match and tournament data (no personal data is shared)

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

9. Data Retention

We retain your preferences (favourites, notification settings) locally on your device. Firebase Analytics data is retained according to Google's standard retention policies. You can clear all local app data at any time by uninstalling the app.

10. Children's Privacy

Scorexi is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of personalised advertising
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us via our website.

12. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We use Firebase's built-in security features and follow Google's recommended security practices.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through an in-app announcement. The updated policy will always be available within the app and on our website. Continued use of Scorexi after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
